London is the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom. The city is located near the Thames Estuary in South East England. Londinium was founded by the Romans almost 2,000 years ago. The City of London, the historic core of London with an area of just 1.12 square miles (2.9 km2) retains borders that closely follow its medieval boundaries. London is governed by the Mayor of London and the London Assembly.
